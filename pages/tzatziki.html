Mom's Tzatziki

Back to main page

Ingredients

  • 1kg full fat greek yogurt
  • 1 cucumber
  • 3-4 garlic cloves
  • Olive oil
  • White vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

    • Instructions

    1. Chop cucumber and drain off its water in a colander for 15m
    2. Chop garlic
    3. Put salt, pepper, vinegar, and the cucumber in the yogurt
    4. Mix really well
