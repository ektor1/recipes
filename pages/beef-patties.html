Beef Patties

Back to main page

Ingredients

  • 1kg minced beef
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano
  • Sale and pepper
  • Onions and garlic
  • Bread
  • Vinegar
  • Mustard
  • Parsley
    • Sorry no measurements because every time I ask my mom she says "with the eye"

    Instructions

    1. Chop onions and garlic
    2. Put bread in water until soft and break it to small pieces
    3. Put the minced beef in a bowl and mix with all the ingredients above
    4. Let the mix rest for a bit
    5. Shape the patties
