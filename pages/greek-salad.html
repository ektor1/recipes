Greek Salad

Back to main page

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Red onions
  • Feta
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Oregano
  • (Optional) Balsamic vinegar or red vinegar

    • Instructions

    1. Chop cucumber, tomatoes, and onions
    2. Add salt and olive oil
    3. Mix well
    4. Place feta on top
    5. Pour some olive oil on top of the feta
    6. Make it rain with oregano
